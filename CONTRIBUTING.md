# Contributing

Thanks for taking the time to contribute to `DynBufReader`.

## Licensing of contributions

The public release of `DynBufReader` is distributed under `AGPL-3.0-only`. The
maintainer may also grant narrowly-scoped alternative licenses on request, as
described in [ALTERNATIVE-LICENSE.md](ALTERNATIVE-LICENSE.md).

Because of that second point, contributions are accepted only under the
contributor license agreement in [CLA.md](CLA.md). The CLA is what
lets the maintainer keep the public release copyleft while still being able
to grant the kind of exceptions described in `ALTERNATIVE-LICENSE.md` — a
plain inbound=outbound GPL flow would not allow that.

By submitting a pull request, patch, or other intentional contribution, you
are representing that you have authority to submit it and agreeing to the
terms in [CLA.md](CLA.md), unless the maintainer has agreed with you in
writing to use a different contribution process. There is nothing to sign —
the pull request template includes a checkbox as a reminder, and opening the
PR is itself acceptance.

If you are contributing on behalf of an employer or client, make sure you
have the necessary approval before submitting anything.

Whether the CLA applies to a given change is governed by the definition of
"Contribution" in [CLA.md](CLA.md) § 1. Changes that fall outside that
definition — such as typo fixes and whitespace changes — do not require the
CLA. See CLA.md § 1 for the full definition.
